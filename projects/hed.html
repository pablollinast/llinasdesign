The Work

Three domains.
One mission.

01

Surgical Devices

Developing medical devices that reimagine how surgeons interact with patients. From debridement tools to precision instruments, each device emerged from deep clinical observation and iterative prototyping with biomedical engineers.

02

Medical Training

Designing simulation systems and educational tools that help medical professionals build skills in safe, repeatable environments. Reducing the gap between classroom and operating room through thoughtful UX and physical design.

03

Enterprise Healthcare

Building digital products and systems for healthcare institutions — from workflow tooling to patient-facing interfaces. Bringing design thinking into environments where clarity and precision are critical.

Brand Identity

A mark built from molecules

The HED logo mark is a 3D interpretation of molecular structure — spheres intersecting and bonding, evoking the language of biology and engineering. A visual metaphor for the company's mission: making the invisible visible, and the complex human.
