Curva

Overview

An exploration of fluidity, balance, and ergonomics. Curva emerged from a series of geometric studies, iterating through dynamic curves and intersecting surfaces until form and function became indistinguishable.

The design explores a single continuous element, a band that bends, folds, and resolves itself into a seat. Structure becomes ornament. Constraint becomes expression.
