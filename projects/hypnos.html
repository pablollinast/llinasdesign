Medical Device · 2021

Hypnos®

Overview

After hip replacement surgery, patients face one of the most disruptive challenges of recovery, how to sleep safely. Lying on the side risks rotating the hip joint before it has fully healed, often leading to dislocation.

Hypnos® is an ergonomic brace designed to make that rest possible. Made from high-density memory foam and pressure-relief technology, it holds the legs in parallel alignment, keeping the hip in a neutral position throughout the night, quietly preventing harm while the body heals.
